Agentic AI — Autonomous & Auditable Agents

We design, build and deploy tool-enabled agents that reason, call services, and safely automate multi-step workflows. From a focused POC to production-grade systems with auditability, observability and escalation controls.

What You Receive POC in 2–4 weeks

  • Agent POC & Demo: A working prototype that completes a defined workflow end-to-end (retrieval → reasoning → tool call → response).
  • Integration Adapters: Secure connectors to one or two target systems (APIs, databases, calendar, EHR via HL7/FHIR where applicable).
  • Safety & Guardrails: Prompt constraints, policy checks, deterministic tool behaviors and human-in-loop escalation flows.
  • Observability: Decision logs, explanation traces, and a basic monitoring dashboard for task success / failure rates.

Scope & Timeline

POC: 2–4 weeks. Production hardening: additional 4–8 weeks depending on integrations, audit needs, and scale.

Typical investment

POC: $8k–$20k. Production: $25k–$75k+ depending on compliance, hosting and integration complexity.
